# Outbound Lead Gen: 7-Day Strategy Assets

This document contains the exact, battle-tested templates for your 7-day outbound sprint. **Do not modify the core psychology of these templates.** They are designed to trigger a specific response: "Tell me more."

## 1. Upwork "Sniper" Proposals

**Use Case:** Apply within 15 minutes of the job posting. Only bid on clients with a proven history of spending ($10k+).
**Target Jobs:** Next.js, AI Chatbot, Twilio Integration, Healthcare Web Design, Custom CRM.

**Template A: The "Similar Project" Pitch**
> Hi [Client Name or "there" if name unknown],

> 
> Let's hop on a 10-min call today to discuss the technical architecture. I can show you the backend of what we built.
> 
> Best,
> Ashraf Kamal, Smile Fotilo

## 2. Reddit / Facebook "Value Bomb" Posts

**Use Case:** Post in B2B groups (e.g., r/Entrepreneur, Local Business Marketing Facebook Groups). **Never post links directly unless asked.** Let them DM you.

**Template: The Tear-down / Case Study**
> **Title: How we helped a local Texas business recover $4k/month from missed calls using simple AI**
> 
> Hey everyone, 
> 
> I wanted to share a quick technical breakdown of a system we recently deployed that solved a massive leak for a local business client. They were losing about 20% of new leads simply because they couldn't answer the phone during rush hours.
> 
> **The Fix:**
> We built a lightweight "Growth Autopilot" using Twilio and Next.js.
> 1. When a call is missed, Twilio instantly triggers a webhook.
> 2. Our server pings the user with a personalized SMS/WhatsApp: *"Hi! Sorry we missed your call. We're busy with a client right now. How can we help you today?"*
> 3. An AI agent handles the basic triage and books the appointment directly into their calendar.
> 
> **The Result:** 14 recovered clients in month 1, zero extra human effort.
> 
> You don't need a massive enterprise CRM for this. Just a solid API webhook and a smart conversational flow. If anyone wants to know how to map this out for their specific clinic or service business, drop a comment or DM me. Happy to share the architecture diagram for free.

## 3. The Loom "Video Audit" Script

**Use Case:** Send via LinkedIn DM or Cold Email to high-ticket E-commerce stores or established local businesses with slow websites.
**Tool:** Record your screen using Loom.com. Keep it under 2.5 minutes.

**Script:**
> "Hey [Name], Ashraf here from Smile Fotilo. I was just checking out [Website Name] — love the brand and the new [Product/Service] you launched.
>
> But I noticed a technical issue that's probably killing your mobile conversion rate. I ran a quick Lighthouse audit (show screen). Your mobile site takes 6 seconds to load the largest element. Industry standard for your niche is under 2 seconds. Every second over 2 drops conversions by about 7%.
>
> (Show the specific issue on screen, e.g., 'These hero images aren't lazy-loaded' or 'There's a render-blocking script here'.)
> 
> The good news is, this is an easy fix. If you implement Next.js dynamic imports or compress these WebP assets, you'll drop that load time to 1.5 seconds almost instantly.
> 
> I put together a quick PDF with the exact steps your dev team needs to take to fix this. Reply to this email/message if you want me to send it over. And if you need my team to handle the heavy lifting, we can chat about that too. Have a great week!"

## 4. Cold Email Sequence for Clinic Autopilot

**Use Case:** Send to USA/UK/Dubai Dentists, Dermatologists, and Med Spas.
**Subject Lines to test:** 
- Quick question about [Clinic Name]'s after-hours
- Missed calls at [Clinic Name]
- Idea for your front desk

> Hi Dr. [Last Name],
> 
> I noticed your clinic in [City] is highly rated. Quick question: what happens when a patient calls after hours or when your receptionist is busy? Industry data shows clinics lose 20% of new patients to missed calls.
> 
> We recently deployed an AI Growth Autopilot for a client in Texas that instantly sends an SMS/WhatsApp to missed calls and books them automatically 24/7. It costs a fraction of a human receptionist.
> 
> Can I send you a 2-minute demo video of how it works?
> 
> Best,
> Ashraf
